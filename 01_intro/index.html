PROJECT IDEA 1: Automatic Paper Shredder

Watching my parents work made me realize how much paperwork they need to read and skim through. It also made me realize how more than 50% of these papers are straight up useless and waste time. To make this boring but essential part of work as efficient as possible (which also makes it as quick as possible) I have come up with a product which is basically an automatic paper shredder. The idea with this product is basically a color sensor looks if the document has a red mark and shreds the papers that have the mark. With this product the workload becomes less time taking and only important documents are left for people to read.

PROJECT IDEA 2: Eggcelent Sensor

Tired of not having a healthy breakfast because there are no eggs left in the fridge? This product provides an eggcelent solution to your breakfast problems. By inserting the product into your fridge, the weight sensors under the egg hatches detect if there is an egg in the hatch and sends you notifications when there are no eggs left or the number of eggs are a concerning amount. Also the sensors eggspress the lack of eggs with a built in siren that starts shouting when there are 0 eggs in the container for more than 24 hours.
