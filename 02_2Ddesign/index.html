Week 2: 2D/3D Design

After the introduction to the tools in the lab and the programs on our computers this week we finally started to build our boxes and create physical boxes. Our assigments this week were designing our boxes and use the laser cutter to build them, watch a tutorial and follow it, and model 2 household items that can be found in the lab on a 3D modeling program.







Box Design





This is my 2D design of my box on Fusion 360.





Zooming in is recommended to see the engravings.

The idea with this box was to create a spacious box without a lid to hold my creations. I added holders on both sides to make it easier to carry so I won't drop the box accidentally and break the things inside. After I printed the box I realized that it wasn't enough so I printed a slightly bigger version of the same box. On one side there is my name and surname engraved to prevent people from accidentally taking my box. On the other side of the box there is a QR code that leads to this website.
